About Neerog

Neerog is a medical and healthcare service platform which allows users to search, explore and contact with the doctors, hospitals and testing labs around the globe registered on the platform. You can book appointment online for the doctor of your choice, hassel-free from the comfort of your home in just a few clicks without waiting in long-queues to get an appointment. There is also an option for remote consultation, so one can consult a doctor remotely via video-conferencing. Not only that, this platform allows doctors to manage their appointments and hsopitals to manage doctors.
