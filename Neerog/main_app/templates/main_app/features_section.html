About Neerog

Neerog is an online healthcare service platform which allows users to search, explore and contact with the doctors, hospitals and testing labs around the globe registered with the platform. It is a free-to-use and open-for-all platform and you don't have to spend a single penny for registering yourself or your organization on this platform.

You can book appointments online with the doctor of your choice, hassel-free from the comfort of your home without waiting in long-queues to get an appointment. We also provide an option for remote consultation during appointment booking, so one can consult a doctor remotely via video-conferencing. Moreover, you can apply for medical tests in any testing lab of your choice in no time, with just a few clicks.

Neerog has been developed considering the pandemic situation like COVID-19 where there is a need for such a platform to reduce pressure on hospitals and clinics and providing medical services remotely wherever and whenever possible. In Long Term, this project aims to create a large virtual marketplace for users and healthcare service providers which will benefit both of them and give more power to a customer/patient.

For All Users and Organizations

First, click on Register and then enter your details and password to create an account. Then, you'll recieve a verification link from neerog.web@gmail.com on your registered email, follow that link to verify your email. Now, you can Login using your Email and Password. After that you have to enter few more details to complete your account registeration.

For General Users

Head over to Book Appointment in Our Services Menu to select doctor, hospital or testing lab of your choice. You can also search for doctors and other services using Search Bar on the top-right corner.
